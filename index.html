About TEDx BKBIET

TEDx BKBIET is a student-run independently organized

TED is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. Ted believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world. On TED.com, Ted is building a clearinghouse of free knowledge from the world's most inspired thinkers — and a community of curious souls to engage with ideas and each other, both online and at TED and TEDx events around the world, all year long.
